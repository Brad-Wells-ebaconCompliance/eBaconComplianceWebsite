Contractors providing benefits like health, PTO, and 401(k) are eligible and should be taking credits against prevailing wage fringes. That’s smart business.
But what about the remaining fringe wages?

Using a Trust is an IRS-compliant way to eliminate employer payroll taxes on those fringes — without reducing employee take-home pay.
